Overview: Strategic AI Integration

The GoKwik Merchant Success (MS) team strategically integrates cutting-edge AI tools to transform operations and boost merchant satisfaction. Our core goal: empower CSMs as proactive, data-driven partners.

AI use cases heavily concentrate in the "Post Go Live" phase, optimizing relationship management and ensuring consistent value delivery. This positions AI as a critical enabler for long-term merchant retention and growth.

AI: Driving Impact & Efficiency

Data Analysis

AI analyzes data & context for critical trends and actionable insights. It transforms raw data into intelligence, enabling smarter decisions.

Email Communication

Meeting Management

Chat Analysis

Why the Revamp?

  • Onboarding previously felt overwhelming due to multiple products, outdated materials, and unclear ownership.
  • Our primary goal is to streamline onboarding, significantly reduce ramp-up time, boost CSM readiness, and seamlessly integrate new hires with the GoKwik ethos.

What New Joiners Said

  • Old Flow: Characterized by outdated videos, a lack of Quicksight/Shopify guides, and unclear SPOCs.
  • New Flow: Features bite-sized modules, updated walkthroughs, clear SPOCs, and an intuitive format.

Connection

Key Insights from NPS Feedback:

  • Promoters (120): We have a very strong "promoter" base, especially in Overall and CSM NPS, indicating high satisfaction with our core services and customer support.
  • Passives (40): These customers are satisfied but could be converted into promoters with targeted engagement and demonstrating clear ROI.
  • Detractors (5): While a small number overall, the low scores in Product (8.48) and critically in Finance (1.21) highlight specific areas needing immediate attention and de-risking.

Recommended Next Steps:

  • Elevate Promoters: Invite them to be part of case studies, provide LinkedIn shout-outs, and include them in panel discussions at webinars. Identify early adopters for enhanced CR optimization flows or dashboard upgrades.
  • Convert Passives: Conduct in-person MBRs (Merchant Business Reviews), clearly show the ROI view to these merchants, and present a plan of action based on their expressed goals. Highlight quick wins to reinforce perceived ROI.
  • Rescue Detractors: Prioritize Executive meetings (F2F) with identified detractors (Conscious Chemist, Tripr, Fashion Floor India, BabyOrgano) to de-risk any areas and make the right asks to the Product team.
